CEIS106 Final Project


Introduction to Operating Systems


In this course, we covered the basics of Operating Systems and the security behind them. In this project, we went over the administrative tasks executed on a Linux OS terminal for single computers and networks. We discussed the importance of keeping our systems secure on the network and physically secure.
